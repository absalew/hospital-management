Services

  • Emergency Care: 24/7 emergency services for immediate medical attention.
  • Inpatient Services: Comprehensive care for patients requiring hospitalization.
  • Outpatient Services: Medical care provided without the need for overnight stay.
  • Specialty Clinics: Specialized clinics for various medical conditions and treatments.
  • Diagnostic Services: State-of-the-art diagnostic facilities for accurate testing and analysis.
  • Surgical Services: Advanced surgical procedures performed by skilled surgeons.
  • Maternity Care: Prenatal and postnatal care for expectant mothers and newborns.
  • Rehabilitation Services: Physical therapy and rehabilitation programs for recovery and wellness.
  • Mental Health Services: Counseling and therapy for individuals facing mental health challenges.
  • Pharmacy: On-site pharmacy for convenient access to medications.
